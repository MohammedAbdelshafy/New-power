The model

Two engines, one pickup.

Most collection companies live and die on service fees. We don't. Every bag we collect pays us twice — once as a subscription, once as a sellable material. That second engine is where the real margin lives.

Engine 01 · Recurring

Subscription pickups

Households and businesses pay a flat monthly fee for scheduled, reliable collection. Predictable routes, predictable cash flow.

80 EGP / home / month avg
Engine 02 · Material value

Sorted material resale

Once sorted, plastic, paper, metal, glass and used cooking oil are sold to recycling factories and buyers. Cleaner sorting, higher price per kilo.

~30 EGP / kg recovered
How it works

From your door to a recycling factory in four steps.

STEP 01

Separate at home

Keep recyclables in one bag. We tell you exactly what counts — and we make it simple to remember.

STEP 02

We collect on schedule

Our teams run fixed weekly routes by zone. A driver and collector, same day each week, no chasing.

STEP 03

Sorted at the warehouse

Materials are separated into clean streams. Better separation means higher resale value per kilo.

STEP 04

Back into the economy

Clean materials go to recycling factories and buyers. Used cooking oil is recovered separately.
