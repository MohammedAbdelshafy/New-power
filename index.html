The model

Two engines, one pickup.

Every bag we collect pays us twice — once as a subscription, once as a sellable material. That second engine is where the real margin lives.

Engine 01 · Recurring

Subscription pickups

Households and businesses pay a flat monthly fee for scheduled, reliable collection.

80 EGP / home / month avg
Engine 02 · Material value

Sorted material resale

Plastic, paper, metal, glass and used cooking oil — sorted and sold to recycling factories at premium prices.

~30 EGP / kg recovered
How it works

From your door to a recycling factory in four steps.

STEP 01

Separate at home

Keep recyclables in one bag. We tell you exactly what counts — simple to remember.

STEP 02

We collect on schedule

Fixed weekly routes by zone. Same day each week, no chasing needed.

STEP 03

Sorted at the warehouse

Materials separated into clean streams. Better sorting = higher resale value per kilo.

STEP 04

Back into the economy

Clean materials go to recycling factories. Used cooking oil recovered separately.
